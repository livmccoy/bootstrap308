About Me

Hi, I'm Olivia! I'm a senior in college majoring in Media Arts and Design at James Madison University. Once I graduate, I am hoping to travel the world. I have a fascination with exploring new places and cultures and want to experience the Earth's beauty for myself.

Future Destinations

Here are some destinations around the world that are at the top of my list to visit. It is my goal to experience different parts of the world through traveling.

Bali, Indonesia

Traveling to Bali is a dream of mine. I love how the island has a mix of luscious greens of the jungle and rice fields with the coastlines and beaches. It is the best of both worlds and once you're there, it is inexpensive compared to most other island destinations.

Paris, France

Ever since I was a little girl, I dreamed of going to Paris and having a picnic in a park near the Eiffle Tower. I'm convinced that I will make my childhood dreams come true and visit the city of love sometime in my twenties.

Positano, Italy

Since my family is of Italian descent, I've always had an admiration for the country. Positano's gorgeous coast and amazing culture is what makes me drawn to this city in particular. I'm hoping to put the words and phrases I learned in Italian class last year to use!
